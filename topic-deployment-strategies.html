Deployment Strategies

From development to production: scaling AI systems

What are Deployment Strategies?

Deployment strategies are the approaches and methodologies used to transition AI models and systems from development environments to production. They encompass infrastructure choices, scaling methods, monitoring practices, and maintenance procedures that ensure reliable, scalable, and efficient AI operations.

Canary Deployment Implementation

Kubernetes Canary Deployment 
# deployment-canary.yaml
apiVersion: apps/v1
kind: Deployment
metadata:
  name: ai-model-canary
  labels:
    app: ai-model
    version: canary
spec:
  replicas: 1  # Start with 1 canary instance
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      app: ai-model
      version: canary
  template:
    metadata:
      labels:
        app: ai-model
        version: canary
    spec:
      containers:
      - name: ai-model
        image: your-registry/ai-model:v2.0
        ports:
        - containerPort: 8080
        resources:
          requests:
            memory: "2Gi"
            cpu: "1"
          limits:
            memory: "4Gi"
            cpu: "2"
---
# service.yaml
apiVersion: v1
kind: Service
metadata:
  name: ai-model-service
spec:
  selector:
    app: ai-model
  ports:
  - port: 80
    targetPort: 8080
  type: LoadBalancer
---
# canary-ingress.yaml
apiVersion: networking.k8s.io/v1
kind: Ingress
metadata:
  name: ai-model-ingress
  annotations:
    nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/canary: "true"
    nginx.ingress.kubernetes.io/canary-weight: "10"  # 10% traffic to canary
spec:
  rules:
  - host: ai-model.yourdomain.com
    http:
      paths:
      - path: /
        pathType: Prefix
        backend:
          service:
            name: ai-model-service
            port:
              number: 80
